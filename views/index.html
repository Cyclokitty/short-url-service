Cyclokitty's Super Duper URL Shortener

Save long, unwieldly URLs and get back a nice, fresh link. Just add /new/ and your awfully hard to remember URL at the end of the address bar.

Retrieve your saved URLs by adding /db/ and your saved URL and you'll be quickly re-directed to your saved URL.
